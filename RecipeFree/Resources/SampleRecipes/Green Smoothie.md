# Green Smoothie

A nutrient-packed smoothie that tastes amazing despite being bright green. Perfect for breakfast or a post-workout snack.

*breakfast, vegan, healthy*
**2 servings**

---

- *2 cups* fresh spinach
- *1* banana, frozen
- *1 cup* frozen mango chunks
- *½ cup* frozen pineapple chunks
- *1 cup* coconut water
- *½ cup* orange juice
- *1 tbsp* chia seeds
- *1 tsp* fresh ginger, grated

---

Add all ingredients to a high-speed blender in the order listed.

Blend on high speed for 60-90 seconds until completely smooth and creamy.

Taste and adjust sweetness if needed by adding more banana or a drizzle of honey.

Pour into glasses and serve immediately. Garnish with additional chia seeds if desired.
