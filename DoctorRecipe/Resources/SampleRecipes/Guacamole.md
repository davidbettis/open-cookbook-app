# Guacamole

Fresh and creamy guacamole perfect for dipping tortilla chips or topping tacos. This classic Mexican dip comes together in just 10 minutes.

*appetizer, vegan, mexican*
**4 servings**

---

- *3* ripe avocados
- *1* lime, juiced
- *½ tsp* salt
- *½ cup* diced onion
- *3 tbsp* chopped fresh cilantro
- *2* roma tomatoes, diced
- *1 tsp* minced garlic
- *1 pinch* ground cayenne pepper

---

In a medium bowl, mash together the avocados, lime juice, and salt.

Mix in onion, cilantro, tomatoes, and garlic.

Stir in cayenne pepper. Refrigerate for 1 hour before serving for best flavor, or serve immediately.
