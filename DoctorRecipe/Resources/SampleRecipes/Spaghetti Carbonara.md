# Spaghetti Carbonara

Authentic Italian pasta carbonara made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and black pepper. Rich, creamy, and absolutely delicious.

*pasta, italian, dinner*
**4 servings**

---

- *1 lb* spaghetti
- *6 oz* pancetta or guanciale, diced
- *4* large eggs
- *1 cup* freshly grated Parmesan cheese
- *Â½ cup* freshly grated Pecorino Romano cheese
- *2 tsp* freshly ground black pepper
- *1 tsp* salt
- *2* cloves garlic, minced

---

Bring a large pot of salted water to boil. Cook spaghetti according to package directions until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining.

While pasta cooks, cook pancetta in a large skillet over medium heat until crispy, about 8-10 minutes. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute. Remove from heat.

In a bowl, whisk together eggs, Parmesan, Pecorino Romano, and black pepper.

Add hot drained pasta to the skillet with pancetta. Remove pan from heat.

Quickly pour egg mixture over pasta, tossing constantly to create a creamy sauce. Add reserved pasta water a little at a time if needed to reach desired consistency.

Season with additional salt and pepper to taste. Serve immediately with extra cheese on top.
